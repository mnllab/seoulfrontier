ABOUT SFi

SFi connects technology, entrepreneurship, and growth.

Seoul Frontier Investment (SFi) was established based on the successful experience of the 'SNU Engineering Technology Unicorn Discovery Investment Association' to discover promising early-stage startups in the deep tech field and build a sustainable investment ecosystem that grows together. SFi will become a hub for technology startups that connects Seoul National University's excellent talent and technological prowess with startups, and strongly supports innovative technology startups to grow into global unicorn companies through proven expertise and networks.
